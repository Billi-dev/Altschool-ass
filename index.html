About me

My name is Ajayi Bobola a student of Altschool Afica with matric number ALT/SOE/024/1161.

BIOGRAPHY

Bobola Ajayi was born on the February 14, 1999 in Ibadan, Oyo state. He is the first child in a family of four with a single brother and native to Efon-Alaaye, Ekiti State, Nigeria. He attended Glorious Future Kiddies School where he received his primary education, and Thywill College, Ibadan where he attained his high-school diploma. As a young boy, Bobola always enjoyed playing football with friends. He was also a sprinter during his early years which led to him representing his primary school during Inter school competitions. As a junior secondary school student, he represented his school in several other competitions like the McMillan competition. Bobola's first aspiration was becoming an astronaut, but upon entering Senior secondary school he decided to go to art class instead. After his external SSCE exams, he applied to study law at Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, but was denied and accepted for a BA in English Language instead. He completed this degree in August, 2022. Born a natural entrepreneur, one of Bobola's present aspiration is to become an expert, freelance backend engineer, and eventually, start his own tech company
